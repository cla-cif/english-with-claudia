Hi there! Thank you for visiting and welcome to English with Claudia.

Here you will find everything you might want to know about me and my teaching method.

I am a full-time English teacher based in Italy. I am a CELTA certified teacher with a BA in Literature and Journalism and Mass Communication and have over 8 years of teaching experience. I speak with a distinct southern British accent and I am fluent in Italian, French and Hebrew.

I have had the opportunity to work with business students, army personnel, private tutors and academics. I can proudly say that l have developed a teaching method that is very adaptable to the needs of each student.

My experience in the corporate world has proved particularly useful when teaching business English to adults. I am very happy to be able to share my knowledge with my students, so that they can not only learn English but also understand the basic principles of business culture in English-speaking countries.

I have learned that each of us has a different learning pace and approach. As your teacher, I will guide you through this process and explain each step carefully.

I will encourage you to ask me questions and be curious about why we are reading texts, doing grammar exercises or practising pronunciation.
