Hi there! Thanks for stopping by and welcome to my website.

Here is everything you may want to know about me and my teaching method.

I am a full-time English Teacher based in Italy. I am CELTA certified teacher with a BA in Literature and Journalism and Mass Communication and have over 8 years of teaching experience. I speak in a clear Southern British English accent, I am fluent in Italian, French and Hebrew.

I have had the opportunity to work with students from Corporate world, army staff, private tutoring and academics. I am proud to say that l have developed a teaching method that is very adaptable to every student's need.

My corporate experience has proved particularly handy in teaching Business English to adults, and I am really happy I can share my knowledge with my students, so they can not only learn English but also grasp fundamental principles of business culture in English speaking countries.

I have learned that each of us have a different learning pace and different approaches. As your teacher I will guide you through and explain carefully each and every step of that process.

I will push you to ask me questions and be curious why we are reading texts, doing grammar exercises or practising pronunciation.
